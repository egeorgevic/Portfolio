About

I sit at the intersection of engineering rigour and design craft — which means the sites I build look sharp and hold up under the hood.

I'm an Engineering and Management graduate from the University of Exeter with a first-class degree, currently working as a Software Engineer Intern building UI components, parametric features and 3D-modelling software for HVAC systems. Alongside my studies I've been freelancing as a web designer since 2023.

My work spans real client websites, a hand-coded brand storefront with its own design system, and technical data platforms. I care about the whole picture: understanding the business, sketching the flow, designing the interface, and shipping something fast, accessible and genuinely useful. Whether you need a site that wins customers or an engineer who can own a problem end-to-end, I'd love to help.
